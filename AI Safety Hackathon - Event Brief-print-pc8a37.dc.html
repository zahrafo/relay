1 · Purpose of the event

A one-day hackathon bringing together early-career researchers and students to build practical AI-safety tooling. The event doubles as a community-growth moment for the London AI-safety ecosystem, giving newcomers a low-friction way to contribute and connect with LISA and Horizon researchers.

Primary goals: ship useful safety tooling, surface new talent, and strengthen the LISA × Horizon partnership.

3 · Programme & speaker

Keynote speaker: to be confirmed — a senior figure from the AI-safety field to open the day and set the challenge themes. Horizon is leading outreach and will handle travel and accommodation.

Format: keynote → team formation → build sprints in breakout rooms → demos & judging → closing social. Mentors circulate through the day.
